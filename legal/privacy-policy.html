Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

This QuickBooks API integration is a personal tool for querying and managing QuickBooks Online data. It is not intended for commercial distribution or use by third parties.

Data We Collect

This application accesses data from your QuickBooks Online account, including:

  • Customer and vendor information
  • Transaction data (invoices, bills, purchases, payments)
  • Account and financial data

How We Use Your Data

Data retrieved from QuickBooks is used solely to:

  • Display information in response to your queries
  • Perform searches and reports you request
  • Support the integration features you use

This application runs locally on your machine. QuickBooks data is not stored on external servers or shared with third parties.

Data Storage & Security

OAuth credentials (client ID, client secret, refresh token) are stored in a local configuration file (.env) on your computer. This file is excluded from version control. All API communication uses HTTPS.

Third-Party Services

This application connects to Intuit's QuickBooks Online API. Intuit's privacy practices apply to data transmitted to and from their services. See Intuit's Privacy Statement for details.

Your Rights

You control access to your QuickBooks data through your Intuit account. You may revoke this application's access at any time in your QuickBooks or Intuit Developer settings.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue in the project repository.
